Abstract: ServiceHub – Local Service Marketplace
Title

ServiceHub: An Android-Based Local Service Marketplace for Connecting Users with Nearby Service Providers

Abstract

ServiceHub is an Android-based mobile application designed to bridge the gap between customers and local service providers by offering a centralized platform for service discovery, booking, and management. In today's fast-paced environment, users often face difficulties in finding reliable professionals such as electricians, plumbers, carpenters, tutors, cleaners, painters, and other local service providers. Traditional methods of searching through contacts, advertisements, or local references are often time-consuming and unreliable. ServiceHub addresses this problem by providing a digital marketplace where customers can easily discover, compare, and book nearby service providers based on their requirements.

The application is developed using XML for the user interface design and Java for application logic and backend processing within the Android ecosystem. The system supports multiple user roles, including customers and service providers. Customers can create accounts, browse service categories, search for providers, view provider profiles, check ratings and reviews, book appointments, and track their service requests. Service providers can register their services, manage their profiles, update availability, receive booking requests, and maintain communication with customers through the application.

The application incorporates a location-based service mechanism to help users identify nearby providers efficiently. By utilizing GPS and location services available on Android devices, the system can display providers based on proximity, thereby improving convenience and reducing search time. A rating and review system is also included to promote transparency and service quality by allowing customers to provide feedback after service completion.

The project maintains a structured database containing user information, provider profiles, service categories, booking details, ratings, and reviews. Authentication mechanisms ensure secure access to user accounts and protect sensitive information. The booking management module allows customers to schedule services by selecting preferred dates and times, while providers can accept, reject, or manage incoming requests.

The proposed system aims to digitize local service discovery and appointment management, reducing dependency on manual search methods. It enhances user convenience, improves service accessibility, and provides local professionals with a platform to expand their customer reach. The application demonstrates practical implementation of Android development concepts such as Activities, Intents, RecyclerView, user authentication, database management, GPS integration, and real-time data handling.

This project serves as a real-world mobile commerce solution and provides valuable experience in Android application development, user interface design, database integration, and location-based services. It has potential applications in urban and semi-urban environments where efficient service discovery and booking systems can significantly improve user experience and operational efficiency.

Objectives
To provide a platform for discovering local service providers.
To simplify the service booking process through a mobile application.
To enable location-based search for nearby professionals.
To implement a secure user authentication system.
To provide ratings and reviews for service quality assessment.
To improve communication between customers and service providers.
To digitize appointment scheduling and booking management.
